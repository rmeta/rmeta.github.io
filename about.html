About Me

I’m Armita Tehranchi, a master's student in Operational Analytics under the supervision of Dr. Amir Ardestani at the University of British Columbia. My research spans consumer behavior, machine learning, collaborative consumption, and remanufacturing. I enjoy discovering new possibilities and solving creative problems.

I’ve collaborated with Lululemon to explore younger generations' perception of sustainable fashion. Besides academic pursuits, I enjoy solving puzzles, playing the piano, and student community leadership. Learn more in my CV.
