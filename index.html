About Me

I’m Armita Tehranchi, a master's student in Statistical Machine Learning under the supervision of Dr. Amir Ardestani-Jaafari at the University of British Columbia. I received my B.Sc. with second-class honors from the University of Tehran, majoring in Engineering Science with a sub-field in Computer Engineering.

I’ve had the opportunity to collaborate with Lululemon to explore sustainable fashion using machine learning techniques to analyze consumer behavior. My research incorporates robust optimization to ensure reliable insights into the key drivers of sustainable purchasing decisions, even with uncertain data.
